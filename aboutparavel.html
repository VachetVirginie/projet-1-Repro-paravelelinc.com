Background



Friends since ’95, we’ve been collaborating since 2002 and formed Paravel in 2006. We love the web and working on projects that allow us to push its limits. Recently, our focus has been on responsive, device agnostic design, componentized systems, consulting & prototype building, performance, and product design.
